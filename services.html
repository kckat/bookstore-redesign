Bulk Orders

Parnassus Books can facilitate book orders for all your bulk order needs. Whether quantities of a book are needed for your business's employees, attendees at a conference, or as gifts for clients, associates, or volunteers, we would be glad to work with you on your order. We can ship your order directly to you or make it available for in-store pick-up..

Due to order processing and shipping time, we require 3 to 5 weeks notice to ensure we can make your books available by the time you need them.

We are able to offer special discounts for bulk orders, as shown below. Some exceptions apply. We will confirm all discounts before you place your order with us.
